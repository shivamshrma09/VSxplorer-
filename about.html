About VSXplorer

VSXplorer is a modern web tool to explore, compare, and bookmark the best VS Code extensions from the Open VSX marketplace.
  • Discover top extensions by category (Frontend, Backend, Themes, Productivity, etc.)
  • Search any extension by publisher/name
  • Bookmark and manage your favorite extensions
  • Compare extensions side-by-side

  • Category-wise Browsing: See top extensions grouped by Frontend, Backend, Themes, and more.
  • Instant Search: Find any extension using publisher/extension format.
  • Bookmarks: Save your favorite extensions for quick access. Bookmarks are stored locally in your browser.
  • Comparison: Compare two or more extensions feature-by-feature.
  • Responsive Design: Looks great on desktop and mobile with a clean, modern UI.
  • Hero Section: Engaging hero card for a premium feel on mobile and desktop.

VSXplorer helps you find extensions in popular categories:
  • Frontend: React, Vue, Angular, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind, Bootstrap, etc.
  • Backend: Python, Node.js, Java, Go, PHP, Django, Flask, Express, etc.
  • Themes: Dracula, One Dark, Night Owl, Material, Monokai, Nord, Tokyo Night, Gruvbox, Solarized, Palenight, Synthwave, Catppuccin, Cobalt, and more.
  • Productivity: Prettier, ESLint, Git, Snippets, Live Server, Docker, Markdown, REST Client, etc.
  • And many more!
Tip: Try searching for your favorite theme or tool to see details and bookmark it for later!

  • Fully responsive and touch-friendly design
  • Large hero card for a premium look on mobile
  • Easy navigation and quick access to bookmarks
  • Accordion and cards are optimized for small screens

  • No login required, no data sent to any server.
  • Your bookmarks and preferences are stored only in your browser (localStorage).
  • All extension data is fetched live from the Open VSX public API.
